ABOUT HONDA CBR 250RR

Honda CBR 250RR is an entry-level twin cylinder supersport motorcycle from the Japanese brand. This bike is already available in some of the international markets. It is a very special product from honda, as it performs exceptionally well for a 250cc twin-cylinder motorcycle.Honda has revealed the new 2021 model of CBR 250RR. The new CBR 250RR has a more powerful engine, it now produces 40 Hp of maximum power at 12,500 RPM. It now comes in different colour options and draws its design from its elder sibling CBR1000RR.
